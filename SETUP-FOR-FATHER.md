# Property AI WhatsApp Assistant - Setup Guide

## For Your Father

### What This App Does
- Automatically replies to customer WhatsApp messages about properties
- Helps find properties for rent or buy
- Books viewing appointments and callbacks
- Sends property photos to customers

### How to Use

1. **Open the app** - Double-click the `start-app.bat` file

2. **Connect WhatsApp**:
   - Go to "WhatsApp" page
   - Click "Connect WhatsApp"
   - Open WhatsApp on your phone
   - Go to Settings → Linked Devices
   - Scan the QR code shown in the app

3. **Add Your Properties**:
   - Go to "Listings" page
   - Click "+ Add Property"
   - Fill in property details
   - Upload photos
   - Click "Add Property"

4. **Done!** 
   - The AI will now automatically reply to customers
   - You can monitor conversations in the "Conversations" page
   - View bookings in the "Bookings" page
